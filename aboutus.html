About Our Project

This website was created as part of the National ICT Award 2025 project for Grade 10 Physics Practical. Our aim is to make learning science experiments easier, more visual, and more interactive for students. Through this platform, learners can explore the theory, procedures, and real-world understanding of key physics experiments like Reflection of Light and Concave Mirror.
