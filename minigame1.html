Welcome form our Game(Normal)

HOW TO PLAY

  • 1.Adjust the mirror angle using the slider (or drag the mirror on the screen).
  • 2.Click Fire Ray to send a light ray from the source.
  • 3.Watch where the reflected ray goes.
  • 4.If the ray misses the target, change the mirror angle and try again.
  • 5.When the ray hits the yellow target, you score points and move to the next level.
  • 6.Click Reset Level if you want to restart the current level.
  • 7.You can use Auto Aim to automatically set the correct mirror angle.

Welcome form our Game(Advance)

HOW TO PLAY

  • 1.Your mission is to guide the laser beam from the blue source to the glowing target to charge it to 100%.
  • 2.Drag the BLUE CIRCLE up/down to change the laser height.
  • 3.Drag the GOLD MIRROR to move it around the field.
  • 4.Use the rotation slider to change the mirror angle so the laser reflects correctly.
  • 5.When the reflected beam hits the target, the charging increases. Reach 100% to advance to the next level.
  • 6.Avoid the RED WALL obstacles. They will block the beam. Higher levels include stronger or moving walls.
  • 7.Click "EMP BLAST" to freeze the obstacles for 10 seconds. Then wait for cooldown before using it again.
  • 8.If you need to restart the level, click "Reset Sector".
